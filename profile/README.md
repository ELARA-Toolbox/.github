# Elara Organization

Welcome to **Elara** — Efficient Lie-group Algorithms for Flexible Robotic Analysis and Control.

## About Us

Elara is a research organization dedicated to advancing the analysis and control of flexible robotic systems through efficient Lie-group algorithms and geometric control theory. We develop computational tools and methodologies to tackle the challenges of robots with flexible components, including manipulators with elastic joints, cable-driven systems, and underactuated mechanisms.

## Our Mission

We aim to make sophisticated geometric and control-theoretic methods accessible to roboticists by providing:

- **Robust Algorithms** — Efficient implementations of Lie-group theory applied to flexible robotics
- **Open Tools** — High-quality MATLAB toolboxes for research and industry applications
- **Clear Documentation** — Comprehensive guides and examples for practitioners
- **Active Community** — Collaborative development and knowledge sharing

## Key Projects

### [Elara Toolbox](https://github.com/elara-organization/elara)
A comprehensive MATLAB toolbox for efficient manipulation and control of flexible robotic systems. Features include:
- Lie group and Lie algebra operations (SO(3), SE(3), and extensions)
- Forward and inverse kinematics for flexible manipulators
- Dynamics simulation with flexible components
- Geometric control synthesis
- Trajectory planning and optimization
